How it works

      
Three steps to scaffold your Android locale files and get a ready-to-paste AI translation prompt.

      

        

          
Step 01

          
Pick languages

          
Search and select your target locales from the left panel. Each one generates a res/values-* folder with a blank strings.xml.

        

        

          
Step 02

          
Generate the prompt

          
Optionally expand Source strings.xml and paste your source file. Click Generate prompt to build a translation brief for ChatGPT, Claude, or any agent.

        

        

          
Step 03

          
Download the ZIP

          
Download a ZIP with all locale folders and your TRANSLATION_RULE.md. Drop it into your Android project and start translating.
